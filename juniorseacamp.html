Cost: $1,395.00

It goes without saying that Catalina Junior Sea Camp is an amazing adventure full of snorkeling, boat trips, aquaria, hands-on marine science and the chance to see all kinds of animals in the wild. All of these events are sure to capture your imagination and make for a one-of-a-kind experience! This is only one part of the Junior Sea Camp program, however. Much of the excitement of camp comes from the traditional summer camp activities that you'll participate in. In fact, if you ask return campers they'll tell you that the reason they keep coming back is the camp community, traditions, social activities and the friends they've made!
