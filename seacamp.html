If you're considering attending Catalina Sea Camp, chances are it's the exciting programs of Diving, Sailing, Marine Science and Adventure Courses that's capturing your attention. If you ask return campers, they'll tell you the reason they keep coming back is the camp community, traditions and the friends they've made! At Sea Camp, you'll become a part of this unique community the second you get to Toyon Bay! Throughout your three week experience you will be placed in a crew depending on your age. Five nights at camp are crew nights where you will participate in beach parties, outdoor movies, campfires, karaoke, games, dances and other fun activities. Crew nights change every year so it is always something new and exciting!
