Meet the Team

Administration

Paul

Paul has started his journey at CIMI as a camper. He's worked his way up to directing CIMI summer camps for the past 10 years! During the off-season, he works at Guided Discoveries recruiting campers and staff. He enjoys hiking, paddleboarding and spending time with his wife and 2-year old daughter, Kali.

David

David started as a counselor for Catalina Sea Camp and Junior Sea Camp. He moved up to Assistant Camp Director during the 2013 summer season. He currently teaches Kindergarten during the school year at Crossroads School for the Arts and Sciences in Santa Monica. He enjoys running, SCUBA diving and sun-tanning at the beach.

Miki

Miki joined CIMI this past year as as the Head Counselor for Catalina Sea Camp and Junior Sea Camp. Unfortunately, this is her last year with Guided Discoveries as she has recently accepted a position of Operations Associate at Venture Academy in Minneapolis. She enjoys surfing, fishing and spending time with the CIMI family!
